Our Custom Outfit Services

At Sonali's Sowing, we specialize in transforming your new or old sarees into beautiful, custom-fitted Indian outfits. Simply courier your saree to us, and we'll take care of the rest!

Readymade Nauvari Sarees & Blouses

Experience the elegance of traditional Marathi style with our ready-to-wear Nauvari sarees and matching blouses. Perfectly stitched for your comfort.

One-Minute Sarees

Say goodbye to pleating and pinning! We transform your existing saree into a ready-to-wear, hassle-free One-Minute Saree for all ages. Just wear and go!

Custom Outfit Stitching

From your beloved sarees, we can stitch a variety of custom Indian outfits tailored to your specifications.

Saree Blouse Stitching

We offer expert stitching services for saree blouses, ensuring a perfect fit and style that complements your saree.

Saree Petticoat Stitching

Get your saree petticoats stitched to perfection, ensuring comfort and style for your sarees.

Plazo Stitching

We also offer plazo stitching services, perfect for pairing with your sarees or wearing on their own.

Frock Stitching

Transform your sarees into beautiful frocks, perfect for casual wear or special occasions.

Kurti Stitching

We can stitch stylish kurtis from your sarees, offering a modern twist to traditional attire.

Lehenga Choli (Gopi Dresses)

We also specialize in stitching beautiful lehenga cholis, perfect for festive occasions and celebrations.

One-piece Gown

Transform your sarees into elegant one-piece gowns, perfect for parties and special events.

About Sonali's Sowing

Hello Friends! Welcome to Sonali's Sowing, where my passion for designing and stitching comes to life. I'm Sonali Mokashi, and I'm excited to help you transform your beloved sarees, whether new or old, into beautiful, custom-fitted Indian outfits.

I believe that every piece I create should be simple, elegant, and tailored to enhance your saree's unique beauty. Each outfit is crafted with love and care, ensuring the highest quality and a perfect fit.

Once your outfit is ready, I offer convenient shipping services. I can courier your finished outfit directly to your doorstep, anywhere in India (or outside if needed).
